## Overview

To begin with Dreamscript, sign up for an account and create your first project in the dashboard.

## Steps

1. Visit `https://dashboard.dreamscript.dev`.
2. Register or log in.
3. Click "New Project" and name your project.
4. Start writing scripts in the online editor.
5. Click "Run" to execute your Dreamscript code.

Need more help? Visit the Guides section for tutorials.
